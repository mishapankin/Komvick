Services

  • Financial Audit

    Financial audit of operations on conformity to legislation provisions and interest of owners are necessary to reveal the documents containing possible contempt of law. The basic principles of financial audit are legitimacy, objectivity and independence. The purpose of a financial audit is to ensure that the auditors are functioning correctly. A number of tasks are set to achieve this goal, each of which reflects some aspect of the company's activities.
    In particular, the complex of works may include:
    • Control over the targeted use of funds allocated to recipients
    • Obtaining information on the implementation of projects in the process of their implementation
    • Prevention of inappropriate use of funds of received funds by recipients

  • Tax Consulting

    The main objective of tax planning and tax consulting services is to develop a system in order to achieve maximum financial results at minimum cost, using a set of legal tax minimization methods. Our specialists can develop legal tax ideas for any business by providing individual approach and focusing on specific industry of the client's business.
    Tax support business processes:
    • Consulting on taxation issues arising in financial and economic activities of the company, taking into account the requirements of the current civil, customs, currency and labor legislation representing clients interest in tax audits and in front of other regulatory authorities appeal tax inspection decisions in court and higher instances
    • Consultation concerning application of international tax treaties
    • Advice relating to taxation of foreign organizations doing business in Russia or preparing to start up
    • Preparing of requests to the Ministry of Finance, Federal Tax Service on application of legislation on taxes and fees
    • Accounting policies development for tax purposes focusing on specific industry
    • Methodological support of the tax reporting preparation process
    • Assistance in VAT recovery

    • Accounting consulting consists of professional advice by highly multi-skilled professionals regarding accounting and tax accounting, performed by the company`s accountant.
    • Outsource functions allow companies to concentrate exclusively on their own business, help to reduce financial and tax risks, and to minimize the administrative and financial costs by transfer of task areas or the chief accountant functions to specialist contractor.

    • modern regulatory framework use
    • innovative technologies for training and delivery of reporting
    • no need for interaction with tax or other inspection authorities
    • use of other companies’ experience gained by our employees

  • Appraise

    Appraise consists of detailed analysis of certain areas of activity within a specific project and drawing up a balance of its progress and state in order to improve the quality of implementation in general. Information obtained as a result of audit and project monitoring procedures, as well as obtained through consultations and interviews and related to various areas of project management, containing formalized data is used for appraise. Conclusion containing an analysis of the reasons, as well as recommendations on organizational solutions and measures for the successful development of the project, is prepared based on the results of the appraise.
    The complex of work performed may include measures implementation to assessment of performance of the financed (fund) project in the following areas:
    • As-Is project state, getting results, including its conformity to the declared business plan figures
    • Equipment
    • Practical experience on production and sales
    • Professional evaluation of intellectual property (legal defense on-hand, cost estimate, registration) and ways of use
    • Actual commercialization of project results
    • Reach of company`s development level
    • Identification of potentially patentable results obtained in the course of project implementation and the level of their registration and legal defense
    • analysis of the financial and production company`s activities
